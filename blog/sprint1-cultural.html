What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

A technical post by Cam.

When you first start to play around with CSS you will come to understand that everything (text, images, all content) has a box around it. You may not see this box at first, but you will soon praise this box.

This ‘box’ is made up of a few different things…

The Content

The content is whatever it is you have inserted into your HTML. It could be some text saying “Hello world” or maybe an image of a gazebo. This content can be sized with the width and height properties.
